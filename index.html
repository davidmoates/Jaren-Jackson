Jaren Jackson, Jr | #13

Power Forward / Center

6-11, 242lb (211cm, 109kg)

Michigan State University

Jaren Jackson, Jr. is a 19 year old Forward/Center who is going into his second year in the NBA. He is the son of former NBA champion, Jaren Jackson. Jackson, Jr. played 1 year at Michigan State University where he was Big Ten Defensive Player of the Year, Big Ten Freshman of the Year, Third-team All Big Ten, and selected for the Big Ten All-Freshman Team. He was drafted 4th overall int the 2018 NBA Draft. In his rookie year he averaged 13.8 PPG, 4.7 RPG, 1.1 APG 1.4 BPG with Field Goal % of 50.6. Jackson, Jr. was selected for the NBA All-Rookie First Team.
